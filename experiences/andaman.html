Radhanagar Beach

Radhanagar Beach, located on Havelock Island, is renowned for its stunning sunsets and crystal-clear turquoise waters. It's often ranked among the best beaches in Asia.

Location: Havelock Island, Andaman and Nicobar Islands

Activities: Swimming, sunbathing, beach volleyball

Best Time to Visit: November to April

Ross Island

Ross Island, once the administrative headquarters of the British in the Andaman Islands, is now a ghostly ruin with remnants of colonial buildings and an eerie atmosphere.

Location: Port Blair, Andaman and Nicobar Islands

Activities: Exploring ruins, bird watching, nature walks

Best Time to Visit: October to May

Scuba Diving

The Andaman Islands offer some of the best scuba diving sites in India, with vibrant coral reefs, diverse marine life, and excellent visibility. Popular dive sites include Havelock Island, Neil Island, and North Bay Island.

Location: Various dive sites across the Andaman Islands

Dive Centers: Multiple dive operators offer courses and guided dives

Tip: Try to spot colorful coral reefs, sea turtles, and reef sharks underwater.

Cellular Jail Light and Sound Show

The Cellular Jail in Port Blair hosts a captivating light and sound show every evening, narrating the history of the Andaman Islands and the struggles of Indian freedom fighters imprisoned here.

Location: Cellular Jail, Port Blair

Show Timings: English Show - 6:00 PM to 7:15 PM, Hindi Show - 7:30 PM to 8:45 PM

Entry Fee: INR 100 for adults, INR 50 for children (above 5 years)

Elephant Beach

Elephant Beach, situated on Havelock Island, is known for its pristine white sands, clear blue waters, and vibrant coral reefs. It's an ideal spot for snorkeling, swimming, and beach picnics.

Location: Havelock Island, Andaman and Nicobar Islands

Activities: Snorkeling, swimming, banana boat rides

Best Time to Visit: December to April
