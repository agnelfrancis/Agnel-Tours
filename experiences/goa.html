Goa

Explore the vibrant culture and beautiful beaches of Goa.

Baga Beach

Baga Beach is one of the most popular beaches in Goa, known for its lively atmosphere, water sports, and beach shacks serving delicious seafood. It's an ideal destination for both relaxation and adventure.

Location: North Goa

Activities: Parasailing, jet skiing, banana boat rides

Best Time to Visit: November to February

Dudhsagar Falls

Dudhsagar Falls, located on the Mandovi River, is one of the tallest waterfalls in India. Surrounded by lush greenery and rocky terrain, the falls offer a picturesque setting for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts.

Location: South Goa

Activities: Trekking, swimming (during non-monsoon months)

Best Time to Visit: October to March

Anjuna Flea Market

The Anjuna Flea Market is a vibrant shopping destination in Goa, offering a wide range of goods including clothing, jewelry, handicrafts, and souvenirs. It's a must-visit for bargain hunters and those looking for unique items.

Location: Anjuna Beach, North Goa

Timings: Wednesdays from morning until sunset

Tip: Don't forget to haggle for the best prices!

Old Goa

Old Goa, also known as Velha Goa, is a UNESCO World Heritage Site renowned for its magnificent churches and colonial architecture. Explore historic landmarks such as the Basilica of Bom Jesus, Se Cathedral, and Church of St. Francis of Assisi.

Location: North Goa

Attractions: Basilica of Bom Jesus, Se Cathedral, Church of St. Francis of Assisi

Best Time to Visit: October to March

Casino Cruise

Goa is famous for its floating casinos, offering an exciting gaming experience on the Mandovi River. Enjoy a night of gambling, entertainment, and fine dining aboard a luxurious casino cruise.

Location: Mandovi River, Panaji

Options: Deltin Royale, Casino Pride, Big Daddy Casino

Tip: Dress smartly and bring a valid photo ID for entry.

Spice Plantations

Explore Goa's rich agricultural heritage with a visit to spice plantations in the countryside. Discover a variety of spices, herbs, and medicinal plants while enjoying guided tours, traditional meals, and cultural performances.

Location: Various plantations across Goa

Activities: Guided tours, spice tasting, elephant rides

Best Time to Visit: Post-monsoon season (October to February)
