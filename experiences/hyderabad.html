Hyderabad

Explore the vibrant city of Hyderabad.

Charminar

Charminar is an iconic monument and mosque located in the heart of Hyderabad. It was built in 1591 by Sultan Muhammad Quli Qutb Shah to commemorate the end of a deadly plague.

Location: Charminar Road, Char Kaman, Ghansi Bazaar, Hyderabad

Entry Fee: Free

Timings: 9:30 AM to 5:30 PM (Closed on Fridays)

Golconda Fort

Golconda Fort is a majestic fortress built during the reign of the Kakatiya dynasty. It is known for its impressive architecture, acoustic effects, and the world-famous Koh-i-Noor diamond.

Location: Ibrahim Bagh, Hyderabad

Entry Fee: INR 15 for Indians, INR 200 for foreigners

Timings: 9:00 AM to 5:30 PM

Ramoji Film City

Ramoji Film City is the world's largest film studio complex, spread over 2,000 acres. It offers guided tours, live shows, film sets, and a variety of entertainment options for visitors.

Location: Anajpur, Hayathnagar, Hyderabad

Tour Packages: General Day Tour, Star Experience Tour, Premium Experience Tour

Tip: Book your tickets online to avoid long queues.

Hussain Sagar Lake

Hussain Sagar Lake is a large artificial lake built during the reign of Ibrahim Quli Qutb Shah. It is known for its picturesque surroundings, boating facilities, and the towering Buddha statue installed in the middle of the lake.

Location: Necklace Road, Hyderabad

Boating Options: Pedal boating, motor boating

Best Time to Visit: Evening for a stunning view of the sunset

Hyderabadi Biryani

Hyderabadi Biryani is a world-renowned delicacy known for its rich flavor, aromatic spices, and tender meat. Don't miss the opportunity to savor authentic Hyderabadi Biryani at local eateries and restaurants.

Popular Varieties: Chicken Biryani, Mutton Biryani, Veg Biryani

Tip: Pair your Biryani with Mirchi ka Salan and Raita for an authentic culinary experience.

Shopping

Hyderabad offers a unique shopping experience with its vibrant markets and modern malls. Shop for traditional textiles, pearls, jewelry, and handicrafts at popular shopping destinations like Laad Bazaar, Banjara Hills, and Forum Sujana Mall.

Must-Buy Items: Pearls, Lac Bangles, Kalamkari Fabric, Nizam Jewellery

Tip: Bargaining is common in local markets, so don't hesitate to negotiate for the best price.
