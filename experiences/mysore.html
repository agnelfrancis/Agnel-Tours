Mysore

Discover the royal city of Mysore.

Mysore Palace

Mysore Palace is a very good place but can get crowded in the footwear zone. It is recommended for people arriving in personal vehicles to put their footwear in vehicles and enter directly. Inside, it is also crowded but manageable. Protect your belongings.

Tip: Visit early in the morning to avoid large crowds and to capture beautiful photographs with better lighting.

St. Philomena's Church

St. Philomena's Church is an old and majestic church known for its stunning Gothic architecture and serene atmosphere. It is one of the tallest churches in India.

Tip: Visit during service hours for a unique experience, and don't forget to admire the stained glass windows depicting biblical scenes.

Ajwa Family Restaurant

Ajwa Family Restaurant offers great food, known for its delicious halal dishes. However, it has limited seating, so it's best to arrive early to avoid the crowd.

Tip: Try their signature biryani and kebabs for an authentic taste.

Forum City Mall

Forum City Mall is a great spot for those interested in shopping, fast food, and a variety of local and international cuisines.

Tip: The mall is particularly lively in the evenings and weekends, so plan your visit accordingly if you prefer a quieter experience.

Mysore Zoo

Mysore Zoo is a large zoo covering approximately 3 km, featuring a wide variety of animals, including some rare species not found elsewhere in India and Asia.

Tip: Wear comfortable shoes for walking, and visit early in the day to avoid the heat and crowds.

Lokaranjan Underwater Zoo

Lokaranjan Underwater Zoo is a good one but short. It features many fishes and activities, including a tunnel where you can see fishes from all sides.

Tip: Perfect for a quick visit, and kids will especially enjoy the tunnel experience.

Tipu Sultan's Summer Palace

Tipu Sultan's Summer Palace is a beautiful historical site. Entry requires a ticket, which can be purchased via a QR code on-site.

Tip: Visit during weekdays to avoid the weekend rush and enjoy a more peaceful experience.

Tipu Sultan's Fort

Tipu Sultan's Fort consists mostly of ruins, with little to see. It's more of a historical landmark than a tourist attraction.

Tip: Combine this visit with other nearby attractions to make the most of your time in Mysore.

Tipu Sultan's Death Place

Tipu Sultan's Death Place is a small site featuring his tomb. It's a significant historical spot but doesn't require much time to visit.

Tip: Visit if you're interested in history, but manage your expectations as it is a simple site.

Payana Car Museum

The Payana Car Museum showcases a remarkable collection of cars owned by a passionate collector. It's an excellent place for car enthusiasts and critics alike.

Tip: Allocate at least an hour to explore the extensive collection and read the detailed information about each car.

Chamundi Hills

Chamundi Hills is famous for its Chamundeshwari Temple, offering a panoramic view of Mysore, including the Mysore Palace. It is a must-visit destination despite being a bit farther than other attractions.

Tip: Visit early in the morning to avoid the heat and the crowds, and don't miss the giant Nandi statue on the way up.

Underground Water Jail

The Underground Water Jail is a fascinating historical site where prisoners were once kept in harsh conditions. The jail's structure is partly submerged, making it an intriguing place to explore.

Tip: A guide can provide valuable insights into the history and significance of this site.

Ranganthittu Bird Sanctuary

Ranganthittu Bird Sanctuary is a beautiful place filled with a variety of bird species. It also offers boat rides, though these can be crowded. The bird population varies by season.

Tip: Visit during the early morning or late afternoon for the best bird-watching experience, and don't forget your binoculars.

Brindavan Gardens

Brindavan Gardens is renowned for its well-maintained landscapes and the impressive musical fountain show. The gardens are connected by a picturesque bridge.

Tip: The evening musical fountain show is a highlight, so plan your visit to coincide with this event.
