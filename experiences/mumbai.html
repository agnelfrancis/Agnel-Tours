Mumbai

Explore the vibrant city of Mumbai.

Gateway of India

The Gateway of India is an iconic landmark in Mumbai, overlooking the Arabian Sea. It was built in 1924 to commemorate the visit of King George V and Queen Mary to Mumbai.

Location: Apollo Bandar, Colaba, Mumbai

Entry Fee: Free

Timings: Open 24 hours

Marine Drive

Marine Drive, also known as the Queen's Necklace, is a picturesque promenade in South Mumbai. It offers stunning views of the Arabian Sea and is a popular spot for evening walks.

Location: South Mumbai

Activities: Jogging, walking, enjoying street food

Best Time to Visit: Evening

Bollywood

Mumbai is the heart of the Indian film industry, commonly known as Bollywood. You can take a Bollywood tour to visit famous film studios, watch live film shootings, and even meet Bollywood celebrities.

Location: Various film studios in Mumbai

Tour Options: Studio tours, film city tours, Bollywood theme parks

Tip: Book your Bollywood tour in advance for the best experience.

Elephanta Caves

The Elephanta Caves are a UNESCO World Heritage Site located on Elephanta Island in Mumbai Harbour. These ancient caves feature beautifully carved sculptures and rock-cut temples dedicated to Lord Shiva.

Location: Elephanta Island, Mumbai Harbour

Entry Fee: INR 40 for Indians, INR 600 for foreigners

Timings: 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM (Closed on Mondays)

Street Food

Mumbai is famous for its vibrant street food culture. Don't miss trying local delicacies like Vada Pav, Pav Bhaji, Bhel Puri, and Mumbai-style Chaat from roadside stalls and food markets.

Popular Areas: Juhu Beach, Chowpatty Beach, Mohammed Ali Road

Tip: Explore Mumbai's street food scene with a local guide for the best culinary experience.

Shopping

Mumbai offers a wide range of shopping experiences, from bustling street markets to high-end malls. Shop for clothing, accessories, handicrafts, and souvenirs at popular shopping destinations like Colaba Causeway, Linking Road, and Phoenix Marketcity.

Best Buys: Ethnic wear, jewelry, handicrafts, antiques

Tip: Bargain hard at street markets to get the best deals.
