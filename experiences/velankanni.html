Velankanni

Explore the serene beauty and peaceful atmosphere of Velankanni.

Basilica of Our Lady of Good Health

The Basilica of Our Lady of Good Health, also known as the Velankanni Church, is a renowned Catholic pilgrimage site dedicated to Mother Mary. It attracts millions of devotees from around the world each year.

Location: Velankanni, Tamil Nadu

Features: Main shrine, museum, prayer halls

Annual Festival: Velankanni Feast (August-September)

Velankanni Beach

Velankanni Beach is a pristine stretch of coastline located near the Basilica. It offers a tranquil setting for relaxation and meditation. Visitors can enjoy scenic views of the sea and indulge in beach activities.

Location: Near Velankanni Church

Activities: Beachcombing, sunset views, leisure walks

Best Time to Visit: October to March

Velankanni Lighthouse

The Velankanni Lighthouse is a historic structure located on the coast of the Bay of Bengal. It offers panoramic views of the surrounding area and is a popular spot for photography enthusiasts and nature lovers.

Location: Near Velankanni Beach

Visiting Hours: 3:00 PM to 5:00 PM (Closed on Mondays)

Entry Fee: INR 10 for adults, INR 5 for children

Shopping

Velankanni offers a variety of shopping options for visitors looking to buy religious souvenirs, handicrafts, and local products. Explore the bustling markets and street stalls near the Basilica to find unique items.

Popular Buys: Religious artifacts, candles, rosaries

Tip: Don't forget to bargain for the best deals.

Cultural Shows

Experience the rich cultural heritage of Velankanni through traditional dance performances, music concerts, and cultural shows. These events provide insights into the local customs, traditions, and religious practices.

Locations: Cultural centers, event venues

Timing: Check local schedules for upcoming shows

Accommodation

Velankanni offers a range of accommodation options to suit every budget and preference. From luxury hotels and beach resorts to budget guesthouses and homestays, there's something for everyone.

Options: Hotels, resorts, guesthouses, homestays

Tip: Book your stay in advance, especially during peak pilgrimage seasons.
