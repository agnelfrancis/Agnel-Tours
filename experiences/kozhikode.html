Kozhikode

Explore the city of Kozhikode.

Kozhikode Railway Station

Kozhikode Railway Station is a big and well-maintained station. However, it lacks sufficient shade, so the heat can be uncomfortable during the day.

Tip: Carry an umbrella or a cap to protect yourself from the heat, especially if you need to wait outside.

Kozhikode Beach

Kozhikode Beach is a long stretch of beach with a beautiful view. It's known for the vendors and the lively atmosphere, though there isn't much in terms of sightseeing.

Tip: Evenings are the best time to visit as you can enjoy the sunset, and the beach is cooler with a refreshing breeze.

Mittayi Therevu (SM Street)

SM Street, also known as "Mittayi Therevu," is famous for its wide variety of sweets (mithayis) and other goods like clothing and household items. The street has a vibrant and welcoming ambiance but can be crowded.

Tip: Be prepared for crowds, and enjoy the experience by sampling sweets from different shops. Also, explore the different sections for other goods.

Kozhikode LuLu Mall

Kozhikode LuLu Mall recently opened and is a small but popular shopping destination. Due to its recent opening, it often has a large crowd and heavy traffic nearby.

Tip: Visit during weekdays to avoid the rush, and expect the crowd and traffic to subside after the initial excitement of the new opening fades.
