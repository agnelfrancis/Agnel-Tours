Bangalore

Explore the dynamic city of Bangalore.

Lalbagh Botanical Garden

Lalbagh Botanical Garden is a beautiful green oasis in the heart of Bangalore. It is home to a vast collection of exotic plants, trees, and flowers, making it a popular spot for nature lovers and photographers.

Location: Mavalli, Bengaluru

Entry Fee: INR 20 for adults, INR 10 for children (Weekdays); INR 30 for adults, INR 10 for children (Weekends)

Timings: 6:00 AM to 7:00 PM

Bangalore Palace

Bangalore Palace is a stunning architectural marvel built in the Tudor-style architecture. It resembles the Windsor Castle in England and features magnificent interiors, lush gardens, and antique furniture.

Location: Vasanth Nagar, Bengaluru

Entry Fee: INR 230 for Indians, INR 460 for foreigners (Includes audio guide)

Timings: 10:00 AM to 5:30 PM

Cubbon Park

Cubbon Park is a sprawling green space spread over 300 acres in the heart of Bangalore. It is an ideal place for a leisurely stroll, morning yoga, or a picnic with family and friends amidst lush greenery and serene lakes.

Location: Kasturba Road, Bengaluru

Entry: Free

Facilities: Walking tracks, children's play area, toy train

Bangalore Food Street

Bangalore Food Street is a culinary paradise for food enthusiasts. From traditional South Indian delicacies to global cuisines, you'll find a wide array of mouthwatering dishes served at affordable prices.

Location: VV Puram, Bengaluru

Must-Try Dishes: Masala Dosa, Vada Pav, Gobi Manchurian, Jalebi

Tip: Visit in the evening to experience the bustling atmosphere and try street food specialties.

Shopping

Bangalore is a shopper's paradise with its vibrant markets, upscale malls, and quirky boutiques. Explore commercial street for budget shopping, UB City for luxury brands, and Brigade Road for trendy fashion.

Popular Shopping Destinations: Commercial Street, UB City, Brigade Road, Phoenix Marketcity

Tip: Don't forget to bargain at local markets for the best deals.
